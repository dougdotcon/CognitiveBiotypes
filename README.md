# Depression Research Repository

This directory contains research papers, profiles, and articles related to depression, biotypes, and precision mental health, largely focusing on work from Stanford University and related studies.

## Research Papers & Articles

### Depression Biotypes & Precision Medicine

* **`personalized_brain_circuit_biotypes_depression_anxiety.txt`**
  * *Title*: "Personalized brain circuit scores identify clinically distinct biotypes in depression and anxiety"
  * *Source*: Nature Medicine (Tozzi et al., 2024)
  * *Topic*: Identifies 6 distinct biotypes of depression/anxiety using fMRI.

* **`cognitive_biotype_depression_neural_circuits_treatment.txt`**
  * *Title*: "A Cognitive Biotype of Depression Linking Symptoms, Behavior Measures, Neural Circuits, and Differential Treatment Outcomes"
  * *Source*: Original Investigation | Psychiatry (Hack et al., 2023)
  * *Topic*: Validates a specific "cognitive biotype" of MDD.

* **`cognitive_neural_circuit_biotype_depression_tms_response.txt`**
  * *Title*: "A cognitive neural circuit biotype of depression showing functional and behavioral improvement after transcranial magnetic stimulation"
  * *Source*: Nature Mental Health
  * *Topic*: TMS treatment response in cognitive biotypes.

* **`stanford_study_six_depression_biotypes.txt`**
  * *Topic*: News/Article about the Stanford study identifying six distinct types of depression.

* **`personalized_approaches_treating_mental_illness.txt`**
  * *Source*: Precision Health | Edition 1 / 2024
  * *Topic*: General overview of personalized approaches to mental health.
